# GateOverflow (gateoverflow.in) — Platform Guide

A guide to the **GateOverflow website itself** — its features, pages and how to
use them — so the chatbot can help users navigate and get the most out of
gateoverflow.in. (Site details can change; this reflects the platform as a
Question2Answer–based community for GATE preparation.)

## What GateOverflow is
GateOverflow (GO) is the largest free community-driven Q&A platform for **GATE
Computer Science (CS/IT) and Data Science & AI (DA)** preparation, founded by
Arjun Suresh. It hosts a massive, curated database of **previous-year questions
(PYQs)** with crowd-sourced answers, explanations and discussion. Scale (approx):
~84k questions, ~109k answers, ~291k comments, ~254k users.

## Main navigation & pages
- **Questions / All Activity / Recent** — the live feed of questions and posts.
- **Hot!** — currently popular/active questions.
- **Unanswered** — questions still needing an answer (good for contributors).
- **Tags** — browse questions by topic tag (e.g., `algorithms`, `dbms`).
- **Users** — member list, ranked by points/reputation.
- **Ask a Question** — post your own doubt to the community.
- **Categories** — questions are organised into subject categories (see below).
- **Exam Category** — browse questions grouped by exam (GATE CSE/DA, ISRO,
  NIELIT, UGC-NET, TIFR, etc.).
- **Blog** — articles, interview experiences, announcements, strategy posts.
- **Badges** — gamified achievements earned for activity and quality.
- **GO Timeline** — a chronological view of activity.
- **Chat** — community chat (log in to post); **Chat Logs** archives it.
- **FAQ** — official "how to use GateOverflow" help.
- **Search tips** — documentation for the advanced search syntax (below).

## Previous-year questions (PYQs)
The core of GO. Every question links to a thread with the official answer,
multiple solution approaches, and discussion. PYQs are tagged by exam and year
(e.g., `gatecse-2021`, `gateit-2008`) and by subject. URLs look like
`https://gateoverflow.in/<id>/<slug>` — e.g.
`https://gateoverflow.in/1723/gate-cse-1998-question-9`.

## Advanced search syntax (very useful)
On the search box you can use:
- `tag:apple` — questions with a tag · `-tag:apple` — exclude a tag
- `user:martin` — by author · `title:apple` — in the title · `content:apple` — in body
- `+apple` — force-match a term
- `views:100` · `score:10` · `answers:2` — numeric filters
- `isaccepted:true` — has an accepted answer · `isclosed:true` — closed questions

## Community mechanics
- **Ask / Answer / Comment** on questions (log in first).
- **Up/down-vote** questions and answers; **accept** the best answer to your own
  question.
- **Points & reputation** accrue from votes and accepted answers; the **Users**
  page ranks members.
- **Badges** reward milestones (asking, answering, editing, popularity, etc.).
- **Corrections** / **Flagged Posts** — report errors or problematic content.

## GO PDFs, the Book Viewer & hardcopy
- **GO PDFs** — GateOverflow compiles all PYQs (subject-wise and exam-wise) into
  free downloadable PDF books (e.g., "GO PDFs for GATE CSE 2027"). These are the
  same compiled PYQ collections this chatbot ingests.
- **Book / PDF Viewer** (`/book`) — read those previous-year GATE PDFs online in
  the browser.
- **GO Hardcopy** — printed editions of the GateOverflow book can be ordered.

## GO Classes & Test Series
- **GO Classes** — GateOverflow's structured video courses for GATE CSE/DA.
- **Test Series** — full-length and topic mock tests (e.g., GATE CSE Test Series),
  often bundled with GO Classes.

## Tools & utilities
- **Rank Predictor** — estimate your GATE rank from your expected marks.
- **GATE CSE Marks Distribution** — analysis of marks/score distributions.
- **GO Feature Awareness Videos** — short videos explaining site features.
- **Testimonials**, **Refund Policy**, **Terms & Conditions**, **Privacy**,
  **Contact Us**, **Send feedback** — standard info/help pages.

## Sister site

## Common "how do I…?" answers
- **Find PYQs on a topic:** use Tags (e.g., `tag:dbms`) or the subject category;
  or search `tag:dbms isaccepted:true`.
- **Find a specific year's paper:** browse the Exam Category, or search the exam
  tag like `gatecse-2022`.
- **Read the PYQ PDFs online:** open the Book Viewer at `/book`.
- **Predict my rank:** use the Rank Predictor with your expected marks.
- **Ask a doubt:** log in → "Ask a Question" → add a clear title, body and tags.
- **Get recognised:** answer unanswered questions and earn points/badges.
