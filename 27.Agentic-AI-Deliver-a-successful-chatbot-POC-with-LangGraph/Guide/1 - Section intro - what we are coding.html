Welcome to the Development section of this course! Now that we have defined our chatbot’s expected interactions and conceptualized its architecture, it's time to bring it to life with actual code.


This phase is crucial because it is where we translate our design into a working chatbot. The choices we make here, such as how we structure our agent, how we handle user queries, and how we interact with external data sources, will directly impact the chatbot’s flexibility, behavior, and performance.


In this section, we focus on two key aspects:

  1. Implementing the Agentic Framework: We will use LangGraph to define our chatbot’s logic, allowing it to handle different user intents smoothly.

  2. Integrating External Data: Our chatbot needs to access billing data and electricity plans through a database. We will implement these integrations to ensure responses are dynamic and useful.
